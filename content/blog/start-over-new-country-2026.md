## 🧠 The Truth About "Starting Over"

Moving abroad does not reset your life.

It only changes:

- your environment
- your cost structure
- your daily triggers
- your social access

But it does **NOT** automatically fix:

- loneliness
- discipline
- income instability
- decision-making patterns

---

## 🌍 Why People Fail After Moving

Most failures come from 3 things:

### Cultural adaptation shock

You think you understand a country — until you live there daily.

### Legal/visa complexity

Small mistakes become big problems when you are foreign.

### Lack of structure

Freedom without structure becomes chaos.

---

## ⚠️ The Hidden Risk Nobody Mentions

People focus on:

- beaches
- rent
- weather

But ignore:

> "Can I actually function here long-term?"

That includes:

- bureaucracy
- communication style
- productivity environment
- isolation risk

But **only if you choose correctly**.
